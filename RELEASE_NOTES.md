Reverting to stock Tello operation:
- Power down the Tello and disconnect any external modules.
- Physically disconnect the UZ801’s USB power line from the Tello (leave the Tello’s internal wiring intact).
- Power up the Tello. It should advertise its stock Wi‑Fi SSID and accept connections via the documented Tello Wi‑Fi interface.
